Research Study: Customer Service Communication

We are conducting a research study on how people respond to customers in service-related situations. In this study, you will engage in two brief, text-based interactions with customers and provide written responses as if you were a customer service representative.

The study is completed entirely online and takes approximately 10–15 minutes.

You will be asked to take on the role of a customer service representative and respond to customers through a text-based chat interface. You will complete two separate rounds of interaction, each involving a different customer scenario:

  • In each round, you will read a message from a customer. You will type your response as if you are a customer service employee.
  • Each round is independent, so please treat each customer as a new situation.

After completing each round, you will answer a brief questionnaire about your experience.

Earn up to $5 through Prolific platform. Your compensation will depend on the quality, attentiveness, and effort demonstrated in your responses.

Your participation will help researchers better understand effective communication in service settings, which can inform training and improve real-world customer experiences.
