Emo-Support AI: Client Support Simulation

  • You will roleplay as a Customer Support Representative (CSR) for an assigned scenario.
  • You need to resolve the complaints of 3 different clients. Clients will rate the support they received as 1 out of 5 stars.
    You will receive an additional dollar for every star you receive across the tasks.
  • Our prototype AI-coworker, named Emo-Support AI (denoted by this icon: ) will help you interact with the client. It will generate dynamic suggestions after every client message.
    You must read these suggestions and rate them before replying to the client.
  • After each client conversation ends,
    you will be required to complete a survey describing your experience with the client.
